Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2/24/2026

Welcome to DayNest. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

DayNest is a fully offline personal journaling application designed to help users write and manage daily journal entries securely on their own device.

1. Information We Collect

DayNest does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No personal information collected
  • No email registration required
  • No user accounts
  • No analytics services
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No tracking technologies
  • No device data collection
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone, or location

All journal entries are stored locally on your device only.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data created within DayNest (journal entries, moods, favorites, pinned items) is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not transmitted to any external server
  • Not accessible by the developer
  • Fully controlled by you

If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your journal entries may be permanently deleted unless you create a local backup.

3. Internet Usage

DayNest does not require an internet connection to function.

The app does not communicate with any external servers.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any user data.

5. Data Security

All data remains stored locally on your device. We recommend securing your device with a screen lock, password, or biometric protection to keep your journal safe.

6. Children's Privacy

DayNest does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Since no data is collected, the app complies with children's privacy standards.

7. Backup and Restore

If you use backup features:

  • Backups are stored locally on your device
  • The developer does not have access to backup files
  • You are responsible for managing backup security

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the effective date at the top of this page.
